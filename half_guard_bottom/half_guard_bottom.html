Half Guard Bottom

Basics

Position

  • Outside foot over inside foot
  • Back heeling turning in
  • Pressure is belly down and towards us

Priorities

  • Never let them control inside space
  • Do this by controlling far shoulder, far elbow, far wrist and far knee
  • If at any point we lose this, reset and reframe

High knee shield when opponent insists on smashing

  • Frame
  • Slide knee out and up first
  • Elbow to knee contact
  • Frame away to make space

Collar tie from high knee shield

  • Transfer without loosing knee-elbow connection
  • Pressure down using knee and bodyweight, not arm
  • When they strip, replace with other hand and star over
  • "Please make it stop", they said

Reset their knee

  • Full body move, not arm

Off balances

  1. Knee/shin drive with optional knee tap
  2. Knee lever by pinching knees, turning right hard and bridging up slightly
  3. Pushing them back by elbow posting and sitting forward, remembering knee-elbow
  4. Collar tie pull with kick elevator
  • Alternate between these constantly
  • An opponent catching their balance is subject to attacks
  • An (good) opponent in balance will counter you every time

Flattened

  • Push them towards your feet, knee tap, bridge hard
  • Swim top frame back
  • Use high knee shield reset
  • NOTE: Make sure your head is free to move in the direction of bridge or risk rib strain
  • First direction did not work and/or they are driving very hard to the opposite side
  • Elbow clamp and knee lever
  • Follow through
  • Should they base, comb your hair
  • Push away to reset
  • Elbow escape
  • Kip them all over the place
  • Move their knee away and look for underhooks
  • Or reset

Grip strips

Top hand

  • Line up forearms
  • Second hand ready to catch
  • Full body crunch and catch the hand
  • 2 on 1
  • A little twist won't hurt (us)
  • Off balance

Bottom hand pinned

  • Stab it up and out
  • Yank hand out of there
  • Sandwich grip
  • Magic grip

Knee slice

  • Block hip, block knee
  • Extend legs
  • Shoulder stand and push knee back
  • Last second knee lever
  • Reframe

Toe hold

  • Opponent goes for toe hold
  • Dig leg inside his forearm
  • Push and free
  • Reset

Windshield wiper

  • Hip out and insert butterfly hook

Shitty heel hook

  • Grab his heel and pull it away from his behind
  • Point attacked knee out and heel in
  • Pull bottom leg out
  • Throw leg over and look for heel
  • Chase if needed

Scoop grip

  • Do not let them shelf on their shoulder
  • Frame and drive knee down
  • Knee them in stomach
  • Kick out
  • Reset

Double leg

  • Do not let them shelf on their shoulder
  • Frame and drive knee down
  • Knee them in stomach
  • Kick out
  • Reset

Double leg

  • Intercept early and collar tie
  • Control their wrist and situp
  • Post on their knee
  • Push knee hard and snap hard
  • Stand up in base
  • Snap again
  • Front head lock
